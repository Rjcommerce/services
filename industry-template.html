Sector Challenges

Why Traditional Traceability Fails Chemical Brands

Agrochemical packaging faces extreme environmental conditions, high counterfeit incentives, and multi-layered distribution tiers.

LABEL CLONING

Easily Copied 1D/2D Barcodes

Standard QR codes can be photographed and printed onto fake formulations in bulk. Without covert geo-clone tracking, fakes go undetected until crop failure or chemical damage occurs in the field.

CHANNEL LEAKAGE

Distributor Channel Diversion

Subsidized chemical products intended for one state or territory are quietly diverted to secondary markets. Without automated receipt confirmation, sales teams lose channel discipline.

REGULATORY FRICTION

Slow Regulatory Audits

Reconstructing a chemical lot's journey across 5 disparate software systems pulls IT, legal, and plant managers into multi-week war rooms when government inspection notices arrive.

OPERATIONAL COMPLEXITY

Heavy Retraining Overhead

Complex WMS add-ons require months of staff retraining. Smart Epsilon equips plant floor operators and field sales reps with intuitive mobile workflows trained in under a week.
